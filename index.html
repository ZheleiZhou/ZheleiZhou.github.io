Biography

I am a graduate-year Ph.D. student in the School of Computer Science and Technology at Zhejiang University, where I am fortunate to be advised by Prof. Bingsheng Zhang. My reaserach interests include security, privacy and cryptography. I received my B.S. in the School of Mathematical Sciences at Zhejiang University.
