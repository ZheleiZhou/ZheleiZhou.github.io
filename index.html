Biography

Zhelei Zhou is a third-year Ph.D. student in the School of Computer Science and Technology at Zhejiang University, where he is advised by Prof. Bingsheng Zhang. His reaserach interests include security, privacy and cryptography. He received his B.S. in the School of Mathematical Sciences at Zhejiang University.

Contact: zl_zhou@zju.edu.cn; zl_zhou@outlook.com
